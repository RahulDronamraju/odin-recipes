Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

A classic comfort food, the grilled cheese sandwich is a simple yet satisfying meal. This beloved dish features two slices of bread with a layer of cheese melted to gooey perfection between them. The bread is typically buttered and cooked on a skillet until golden brown and crispy, while the cheese becomes warm and stretchy

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1 slice of cheese (cheddar, American, or your favorite)
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Prepare the bread: Butter one side of each bread slice.
  2. Assemble the sandwich: Place one slice of bread, butter side down, in a skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add cheese: Top the bread with the cheese slice.
  4. Cook the sandwich: Place the second slice of bread, butter side up, on top of the cheese.
  5. Flip the sandwich: Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown, then carefully flip the sandwich.
  6. Finish cooking: Cook until the other side is golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  7. Serve: Enjoy your grilled cheese sandwich!
